About

Hi! My name is Michelle Graciela, but you can call me MG. Basically, I love art. I love to sing, dance, paint, write, draw, act, and many more. I'm the current Performing Arts Directress and the Music Head Division in the longest-running theater organization in Intramuros Manila, Tanghalang Batingaw. It's always been my dream to share the world I'm creating through my artwork. Since we're all in a journey called life, hope you can see a glimpse from a piece of my story.
